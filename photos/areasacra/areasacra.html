area sacra di largo argentina
sacred area of argentina

right around the corner from us there are these sets of ruins. we didn't know quite what they were, so we stopped by to see. apparently these are the ruins of four temples and what not, these are the oldest known temples in rome. they also had a political meetings here and different ceremonies. this is also where on, 15 March 44 BC Caesar was murdered.
