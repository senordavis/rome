the janiculum hill
one of the many hills of rome

there are many hills in rome. ancient rome was actually founded on seven hills. the janiculum is not one of the seven, but it offers a spectacular view of the city of rome. if you are up here during the day, at noon the roman millitary shoots a canon every day to commemerate the unification of italy by garabaldi.
