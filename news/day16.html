Day 16
just another day
Wednesday, 4 February, 2004

today was just a good day. nothing to exciting, nothing to hectic or crazy.

we did laundry this morning, came home for lunch, then went back up to school where leanne sat on the computer for a while until she had to go to class.

mike though went to a meeting for the JCU voice, its the school's newspaper/flyer thing. apparently because its a real business orientd school, there aren't to many people that know what they are doing when it comes to the whole design thing, so mike is probablly going to be laying out the whole paper and design and such.

after we went to class we came home had some food, went out and bought some work out clothes cause we have a gym that we can go to for free from the school, that happens to be right around the corner, so we will be going there, and some mornings we will be running.

for the rest of the night we just sat at home and did homework.
