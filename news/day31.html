Day 31
rain and more rain
Thrusday, 19 February, 2004

today we woke up and guess what? it was still raining! yes we know, we know, we have nothing to complain about, but the rain here is just not fun. you get wet, you get cold, you get sick.
but anyways. because it was raining, mike thought his class couldn't possibly be meeting outside today, but they did anyways. so whem mike went up to the classroom and waited for his class, no one showed up. so he went and sat downstairs with leanne until italian started.
nothing to big is going on today, we are just going to vienna for the weekend. and thats really about it. it actually a pretty quiet place, so we are just going there to relax for the weekend.
