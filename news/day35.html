Day 35
back in rome
Monday, 23 February, 2004

just a typical monday. not much to do when we got back into rome.

we went to the apartment and dropped our stuff off and unpacked everything.

leanne went to class while mike waited for her in the lounge. we then did our groccery shopping for the week, and bought stuff for dinner tonight.

when we got home from the store mike started cooking, and people came over at about 7:00, 7:30ish and we had some good bruschetta, pasta, and slightly breaded chicken covered in sundried tomatoes.

from there we went to the gelato shop and got some gelato for dessert.

a good calming, relaxing night hanging out with some friends.
