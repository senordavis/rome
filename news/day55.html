Day 55
just a good day
Sunday, 14 March, 2004

really today was a nothing day again.

we had our morning feast of johnny ce ces again. today we had our friend chrsity over cause she had spent the night.

from there we went up to school and did some homework, and got some things done.

afterwards we went home to a good lunch, and then to church.

after church we went out to a restaurant and had dinner and sat and talked about the readings in church, and the homily. we sat there for a while, and then went home and fell asleep.
