Day 21
getting things done
Monday, 9 February, 2004

today we woke up and ran to the capitoline hill. it was a good run, a good distnace and a great hill to run up. when we got to the top of it, we were quite winded, so we walked a ways before running home.

when we got home we showered and then got ready to tackle the day. we went out to breakfast, and then from there went to the grocery store to buy food for the week. we did a really good job and only got the things on our list.

from the grocery store we were going to buy leanne some shoes, but the shoe store wasn't open and the department store that is on the floor above the grocery store didn't have any shoes. so we just came home and had some lunch and then headed up to school so that leanne could go to school and so that mike could sit on the computer and get some stuff done.

while we were sitting in the lounge before leanne's class, we ran into some of our friends and invited them over for dinner later tonight. they kindly accepted and said that after their class, they would stop buy.

so back to the grocery store we went to get a few more things to make dinner with.

mike had come up with a crazy idea for dessert of taking and gutting some orange halves, and serving strawberries and cream with some spumante in the empty orange halves. this went over quite well, and it tasted really good too.

when dinner was done we all decided that we would have to do this every week, and that we would just take turns for who would cook when. it should be a really good plan.

for the rest of the night we just sat at home and did some homework and went to sleep.
