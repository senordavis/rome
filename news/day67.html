Friday, 26 March, 2004

again a pretty much nothing day again.

mike slept in (again), and then we just kinda lingered around the apartment until it was time to go to school for leannes classes.

leanne had class today because here they have "make up classes." which in is basicly that the professor has canceled class for a day, or either the school has canceled class for a day and so they scheduled a day when the classes would be made up.

so leanne went to class while mike sat in the library. when leanne got out of class, we did some stuff on the computer and found out about a barbeque that the school was having. so we stayed around for some free food.

after hanging out at the school for a bit and doing some more computer work, we went home and went to bed.
