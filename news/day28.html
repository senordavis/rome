Day 28
the train ride home
Monday, 16 February, 2004

well we went to sleep on the train yesterday, and woke up on it today. really we kind of had to, unless we got of while in our sleep.

the train ride went really really slow. we ended up leaving an hour late, and every time we stopped, it seemed to take forever to get going again. we just were hoping to get back to rome on time to get leanne to class.

the rest of the day from there was pretty uneventful. mike spent it on the computer putting up all the wonderful new things that you can see, and leanne was in class, and then did homework for the night.

we decided to just stay in and recuperate from our vacation.
