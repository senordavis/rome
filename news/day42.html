Day 42
a little bit of rain, and a little bit of sun
Monday, 1 March, 2004

today was great! we woke to a wet, wet city. and sat in bed for a bit just talking about what we needed to do this week. then we went to the gym and ran for about an hour.

it was amazing, running on the tredmill and just going for an hour. i know, i know, its hard to believe that us fat people could do something like that for that long, but we did. and we are going to keep doing it until we aren't fat anymore. or at least until we can get closer to being not fat.

from the gym we came home and showered and got ready to go. we were all set to go and then we get outside and its hailing. there was hail today, big hail to. so instead of walking across town to our grocery store we went to one real close to us.

after lunch we went to school to check emails, and for leanne to go to class.

tonight we went out to the residence to have dinner out there with all the friends.
