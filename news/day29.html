Day 29
getting back into the swing of things
Tuesday, 17 February, 2004

you know how everyone says that you need a vacation from your vacation? well they are right. jumping back into things was a bit hectic yesterday, and then today was even worse.

we started off the day with a trip to the roman forum for our art history class. it really is a great place to see alot of ancient rome. granted most all of it is ruins, but there are still some really cool things to see.

when class was over, mike wanted so badly to not go to italian because it was so sunny and nice out, that it begged the question..."what is more important class, or having fun outside?" can you guess what we did?

after italian we walked up to the spanish step to see if they had the DK guide book for Vienna, they didn't but did find some for amsterdam, and florence.

when we got back to the apartment we dropped stuff off, and then headed up to the school to meet up with some friends to go to dinner at their place. we had a tomatoe risotto (its like rice and tomatoes and meat, quite yummy) from there we came home and just crashed.
