Day 13
SUPERBOWL SUNDAY
Sunday, 1 February, 2004

its the big day, superbowl sunday, the biggest and grandest spectical in american sports!

so we had been looking for where we could go to see the game and when we went out on friday night with the girls from state, we found out that the bar that we were at was showing the game. so we planned on going there.

but the game wasn't starting until 12:25am local time, so we had some time to kill. in the morning we went to mass at the vatican again and went to high mass, which was in latin, and we stayed afterwards to see the pope.

from there we went to the school and sat there doing homework until they closed at 7pm and then had dinner. at about 10:30pm we headed down to the bar to get a place so that we could sit and watch the game.

we met up with some people from our group from state, and while there, we met some other students and people that are here studying from other universities.

overall it was a good night.
