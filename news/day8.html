Day 8
a trip to the spanish steps
Tuesday, 27 January, 2004

well as the semester gets started, more and more it seems like this is really going to be school rather than a vacation with some time where we are required to meet at these random places called classrooms.

now being serious, school is going wonderfully. we started italian today and although leanne says that she's in it only because she has to be, it's something new for her and she will do well, and maybe enjoy it alittle. mike loves it and can't wait for more. our history of art class from this morning was rescheduled for friday so that we can get everything together and know where to go before next week seeing as in how there is no classroom.

this morning we went to the spanish steps. because we had to go to the book store up there to get books for classes and what not. there was some really cool stuff around there, but we were on a mission for books and that was it. maybe we'll come back and do the shopping thing later.

later we are going to go to the market get some food and stuff for dinner.
