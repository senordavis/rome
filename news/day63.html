Day 63
dinner with the friends
Monday, 22 March, 2004

so today we still didn't have hot water.

as a recap, or maybe as a first, we didn't have hot water when we came home from florence.

but we dealt. we got ready for school had some breakfast, and went off to school. we sat in the library, and just hung out doing some different work. leanne read about venice and what we are going to do this weekend. we did some work on the computer and uploaded pictures to our shutterfly account. (its a nice little digital pictures management service)

while sitting there some people from leannes class came down (before class) and let her know that class was canceled for the week. so we then finished up what we were doing, and went home for lunch.

we started cooking a little bit later, and slowly everyone began arriving for the feast of bruschetta and spaghetti. we hung out, talked, laughed, and then they all went home. leanne called her mom, and then after that we went to bed.
