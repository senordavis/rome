Day 24
we're going to paris
Thursday, 12 February, 2004

today is the day, the first day of traveling in Europe!

we actually got really lucky and for our italian class today we had a quiz, but that was it. we were able to leave right after it.

not much done before that. mike had class today at the pantheon. and we had some people over for lunch, but that was really the extent of the day.

most of these updates and galleries are actually being done on the train ride to paris.

we are so excited, and can't wait to get there.
