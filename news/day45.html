Day 45
midterm reviews, and sight seeing
Thursday, 4 March, 2004

today mike had his midterm review for his other history of art class, and it was completely pointless.

after the waste of time of a class, we went to italian. italian itself was an adventure. the opposite side from where we sit is comprised of people that seem very unintrested in the class, and do almost everything to keep us from learning.

after class, we went home to drop of books, and then went off to meet the davis family at the vatican. they had spent the morning there in the museums and in the church. we took advantage of the time that we were up there to send out some mail that we have had at the apartment that needed to be sent out.

we got together with them and then walked back to the apartment, and on the way stopped at piazza navona, and at the pantheon.

after stopping by these, we came back to the apartment and cooked some italian food. for them.

after dinner mike walked the davis's up to the colosseum metro stop and sent them back to the hotel.
