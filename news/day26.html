Day 26
a valentines day walk around paris
Saturday, 14 February, 2004

well its valentine, the day of love. and we are in Paris, the city of love, so what else do you do in the city of love other than go to the eiffel tower and kiss under it? not to much else.

we went to the Louvre today and looked through lots and lots of art. its a really bif museum, and we got through most of it. but decided after seeing the mona lisa, and noticing that it was getting pretty crowded, and that we'd seen most all that we wanted to, we left.

we got outside and it was a bit cold, but we as michagnanders could deal and walked to the tower from there. we got to it took some pictures, kissed underneath of it. You know the usual lovers in paris thing.

from there we walked across the street and up the steps to the place where everyone takes their picture at, and helped out some other people with taking their pictures. after sitting there for a while we decided to go to hard rock cafe. we weren't very hungry, but we ate anyways. we got these really good nachos, and a great burger, and then a plate of dessert nachos, which are these cinnamon and sugar chips with different fruit on top, ice cream in the center, and then chocolate syrup drizzeld all over it. quite yummy i would say.

after dinner, we headed back to the tower to see it at night, which was gorgeous by the way. went up to the top to see the view of paris from up there. after we had done all the toursity things for the day we headed back to the hotel to sleep and prepare for tommorow.
