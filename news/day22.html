Day 22
a day full of class
Tuesday, 10 February, 2004

today not alot of stuff went on. it was a fairly class heavy day. we had our arts and monuments class this morning. from there we went up to a piazza by school, and ate our lunch that we made, and then went to italian.

after italian class, we went up to the school to use the free internet access, and booked most all of our hostels for our weekend trips, and got everything planned for spring break.

from there we just came home and spent the rest of the night at home doing homework, and working on computer stuff.
