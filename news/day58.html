St. Patricks day!
Wednesday, 17 March, 2004

what a wonderful day today will be.

its st. patricks day, we're in rome, and we have nothing to do today.

well really thats not true, but nothing to pressing to do today that can't be gotten done and then take the rest of the day to relax.

because leanne didn't have her hummanities or histroy class today, and mike doesn't have class at all, we decided that today would be tax day. and that's exactly what it was.

leanne woke up early and did taxes. got through all of the information that we had with us here, and then just had to wait until it was late enough here to be able to call mom to get some more information.

from there we went to school to sumbit it all over the internet, and that was it. we had met up with some people and were talking about what to do later tonight. some of them were going to some bars close to them so we were just going to hang out in campo for some of the night and watch everyone else.
