Day 12
getting things done
Saturday, 31 January, 2004

we had a few things to do today, nothing to important though. we went to the books store in piazza di spagna again and exchanged one of our art history books for a different one.

form there we went to stazione termini (the main train station here in rome) and made reservations for our train to and from paris. on our way out, the olsens called and leanne talked to them for a bit. it was very good to hear from them.

after that we walked home, and on our way there, stopped at the coloosseum and walked around there for a while, and then around the roman fourm. on our way home we found where we have to be for class on tuesday.

then we went home and had some wonderful ravioli that we had boughten at the groccery store. and then sat around the arpartment for the rest of the night.

tommorow's the super bowl!
