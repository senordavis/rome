Day 14
a trip to the questra
Monday, 2 February, 2004

so we had to be up this morning at 6:00 am so that we could get to the police station on time for our appointment for the permisso di soggiorno, but even though we thought we would be fine staying out last night apparently we weren't.

we woke up this morning at 8:30 am and had to hurry to our appointment, which was at 9:00 am.

when we headed out from the apartment, we got to the street that would take us to the streee that we needed to go to, but then for some odd reason we went the wrong way and had to turn around.

we got to the station by like 9:20 am and went in slightly worried, but got there and we were fine. we got a visitors permit cause not just anyone can go into the police station. we walked up to the office where we were to get our permisso, and were fine. no real worry about our appointment time. it wasn't really a fixed appointment. we just had to show up today and get everything done today.

we walked out of there a little wiser as to how late one can stay up if you have to be somewhere the next morning.

the rest of the day was spent at school, then to the grocery store, and then home for dinner and homework.
