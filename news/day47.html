Day 47
and yet more sight seeing
Saturday, 6 March, 2004

today we slept in a little, and let the davis's sleep in a little, and we met up with them at the hotel.

from the hotel, we walked down with them to santa maria maggiore. we looked around in there, and headed down to another church, san pietro in vincolli, and were going to look at that one, but it was closed until 3:30. so we went down to walk through the forum. we showed them all the really fun things in there, and it was good for us cause it was like a review for our midterm on tuesday.

after this we went and had some lunch and headed back to san pietro in vincolli. after we had a look at the church, leanne and mother went to go buy some small things to make a snack, and mike took the boys shopping again.

when we all met up at the apartment, mom and mike made some johnny cakes(a bad translation for a "dominican" snack: its bacsiclly just fried dough) and we all munched on those for a while.

after sitting for a bit, we headed out to dinner. doug was so taken by the pizza the the restaurant we went to the first night, we went back there.

when we had sufficiently eaten ourselves sick, we took them back to the hotel and said our goodbyes.

we came home and just crashed.

thus the end of the davis vacation.
