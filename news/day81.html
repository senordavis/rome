Day 81
some more train, and then some sleep
Friday, 9 April, 2004

when we woke up on the train we only had a few more hours to go.

we got to rome at about 8:15 in the morning, and went to mcdonalds to get some breakfast. from there we came home unpacked things, and put things away.

we top a bit of nap to be able to rest and recuperate.

when we woke up, mike made leanne stay home in bed to feel better, and he went and did some grocery shopping, and then he went and did the laundry. he came home and we had some dinner and sat in bed reading for the rest of the night and just chilled.
