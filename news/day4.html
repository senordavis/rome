Day 4
orientation winds down
Friday, 23 January, 2004

so today after sleeping in we got up and grabed something for lunch. we got up to the school and sat around for a bit waiting for everything to start up. we were supposed to go to a library orientation session, but no one showed up to actually tell us about the library.

it was cold again today and we had signed up for a tour of the area that we live in called Trastevere, but decided against it. the cold weather also deterred a few other people. some other people from state that we had been hanging out with asked if we knew how to get the Campo de Fiori, which is where we live, because they really wanted to see it. according to almost everyone, its one of the best places in rome to do just about anything.

we got back to the Campo de Fiori area and they went off to explore while we found a cool little paninni restaurant and sat there to warm up. Leanne had a coke and i got a cappuchino and we ate some really good paninni's.

for the rest of the night we just sat at home and talked for hours.
