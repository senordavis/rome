Day 84
easter monday
Monday, 12 April, 2004

yep that's what it says. today is actually a holiday here in itally. hardly anything is open. really its only like restaurants and a few shops and things like that.

but we decide that because it was a holiday, we would treat it as such. we slept in this morning and just layed around for a bit. leanne got up and went to the kitchen and made some hot chocolate using the left overs from the chocolate eggs that we had gotten for easter.

for the rest of the day we just sat in bed and read. leanne had a lot of reading for school left, and so she did that. mike read another book. his second in an entire week.

for dinner we had some take out pizza. and then worked on photostyle stuff. after a while we just went to bed early.
