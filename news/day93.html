mike speaks a new language
Wenesday, 21 April, 2004

today we woke up to more sun. it was goregous. we headed down to the gym to go work out first thing. after a little bit mike decided that his legs hurt to much from his graceful fall on monday so ohe went home while leanne ran for another 40 minutes.
