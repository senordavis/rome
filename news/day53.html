it all over now
Friday, 12 March, 2004

well today was the end of the offical midterms week here.

but leanne is taking this time now to prepare what she calls hell. (its really the two hardest midterms that she has ever taken in her life)

but thats really all that happened today. mike woke up with a soar throat, went and did laundry, and then came home and made lunch.

after lunch we went and hung out at the school in the courtyard cause it was so amazingly nice out today.

tonight we went down to dinner at kelly and kristene's. they are the two girls who live in the apartment below us.
