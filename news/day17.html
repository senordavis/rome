Day 17
class, no power, and a slow internet connection
Thursday, 5 February, 2004

so today we went running this morning. no marathon yet, just up to tiber island, and then back.

after our run this morning we came back home to get ready for school. while leanne was in the shower the power went out. mike went to check the circuit breaker and went to turn it back on, but when he did the lights all flashed, and then went out.

leanne asked if everything was alright, cause while she was in the shower, the hot water heater started making funny noises.

we weren't really sure what happened, and just proceeded to get ready in the dark. when we got to school, we went up to student services and told them, and they called the maintainance people for where we live and had them come "fix" the problem.

when we came home later that night we did have power, but no hot water heater.

the rest of the day wasn't so bad. we decided to go on trip to Bologna with some friends from school, and were then going to stop into Florence for the rest of the weekend, but because the internet was so slow, leanne could get us a room anywhere, cause by the time she was able to try and book anything, all the rooms where booked.

but tommorow we go to Bologna.
