Day 6
a quiet day in rome
Sunday, 25 January, 2004

this morning we woke up after a night of enjoyment in the italian culture, and a really good nights sleep, and went back to the vatican this morning for mass.

yet again something completly different from what we know. according to our handbook from school the mass was supposed to be in english. needless to say it wasn't, and we sat there knowing what was going on, but not really understanding everything. the mass was also shorter than normal. and there weren't as many people as we thought there would be.

when mass was over we went home, put on some warm and comfortable clothes and layed around reading and working on the computer. nothing is really open today so we spent the day just sitting around and relaxing before school starts tommorow.
