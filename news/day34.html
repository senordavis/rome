Day 34
guess what, more cold
Sunday, 22 February, 2004

so today it was cold again, not much to do today cause just like in rome most everything closes on sunday.

so we just kinda walked around, went to a museum and saw some cool drawings, and then went and sat at a restaurant for a couple hours. then we tried to go to another museum, but weren't able to get in cause we missed the last admission time.

by the time we had done all of this (really nothing at all) it was just about time to get on the train, more so head to the train station so that we could get on the train when it got there.

we lucked out this time in our compartment cause the two girls in there when we got there, spoke english. they were from cinncinati and were studying abroad as well.
