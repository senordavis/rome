Day 11
a walk up the janiculum
Friday, 30 January, 2004

today we had to meet for our art history class that meets on tuesday. for some reason with the whole first week of school thing, there wasn't enough room for us to use a classroom on tuesday so thus we met today.

we sat in class for an introduction and then left for the onsit protion of class. we ended up going up the hill behind the school, the janiculum, and talked about the history of rome. mike again was his normal history of art nerdy self and took tons of notes.

after class we went to see if we could get the sketch books that mike needed for his drawing class, while we were there we ran into some students that are from john cabot. but were very not nice. all we needed to do was get into the row where the sketchbooks were and they two of them stood there like gaurd dogs making sure that we couldn't get in while the other one looked for a sketch book.

we needed to be back up to school by 5 to drop off our paper for the permiso di soggiorno again cause the girl filled the forms out wrong the first time. we sat there for a while waiting for them to finish chit chatting with one another and actually do work.

when we were done with this we went back to the art store and got the sketchbooks mike needed. luckily the girls weren't still there like leanne had joked.

also while we were by the art store the first time waiting for it to open, we found a huge department store that has a huge food store that feels much more like a meijers than anything else that we have found.

we then went to campo de fiori (where we live) and went out to eat. then back to the apartment. we got a call from some friends on the trip with us from state, and went out to bar with them and then home to sleep.
