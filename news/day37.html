ash wednesday
Wednesday, 25 February, 2004

today was pretty much a solem day. we decided to do everything that we possibly can to observe lent. so today we will be fasting until dinner tonight.

other than that not much to do, mike worked on some site stuff, and leanne did some work on her paper for class. then went to class, and then we headed out to the place where she is supposed to tutor.

after tutoring we hopped back on the metro and went off to the residence. we met up with a few friends from there, and went out to dinner for our friend mandy's birthday.

afterwards we took a taxi home. leanne did some homework, and mike slept.
